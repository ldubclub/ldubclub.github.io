Who or what is LDUB you ask? Laci Wall aka LDUB here. “L” for the Laci and “DUB” for the “W” in my last name. I am LDUB and you are the CLUB!

I have a bachelors degree in Human Performance & Wellness and a lifelong passion for music and dance. LDUB CLUB is a balanced blend of dance and fitness. It is a unique, high-energy class, full of positive vibes that I call dance fitness fun! I believe in being active, engaging muscles, building stamina, cardio strength and improving mental fitness. I started as a Zumba instructor in 2010 and that gave me the confidence to eventually do my own thing. I came up with LDUB CLUB in 2011, and my intention since then has been to create a positive comfortable space to “Just Be.”

My path with LDUB CLUB has become more clear after experiencing a multitude of health issues since birth in 1977. To deal with my gut, the insomnia and crippling anxiety growing up, I taught myself to dance and played sports. Dancing was my medicine and my creative outlet that helped me on my journey through life.

JUST BE
When I say dancing helped me, it was the only time that I didn’t have anxiety or feel pain. The music came on, I start moving my body and I feel pure joy! My purpose now, is to share with others the importance of moving your body, freeing your energy and learning to find your worth. I want to be an example of letting go of fear and just dancing it out.

LDUB CLUB is an opportunity for those who have never worked out or danced or feel anxiety. It’s a space to be you and to feel comfortable by doing your version of what I’m doing. LDUB CLUB is for anyone that wants to add a positive healthy element to their routine.

To see someone invest in themselves and transform spirt, mind and body, from something that I subconsciously created for my survival, is one of the most rewarding feelings I have ever felt. For me it’s more than just dance fitness, it’s the connections we make, it’s the way we support each other to be the best version of ourselves that we could possibly be. LDUB CLUB is my way of spreading love in this world.

Thank you for reading my story and look forward to dancing with you soon!
